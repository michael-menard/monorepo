# lnch-1026: Post-Mortem Template

## Context

Phase 2 (Before soft launch).

Post-mortems (also called incident reviews) are essential for learning from failures and preventing recurrence. Without a standard template, post-mortems vary wildly in quality and often omit key sections like timeline reconstruction or action items with owners and due dates. This is a quick win (Size S) — a well-designed template does most of the work. It is referenced by the on-call playbook (lnch-1024).

## Goal

Write a blameless post-mortem template at `docs/operations/incidents/postmortem-template.md` that guides teams through a thorough, consistent incident review.

## Non-Goals

- Do not write a post-mortem for a specific incident.
- Do not document the incident classification system (lnch-1025).
- Do not define who is responsible for scheduling post-mortems (team process).

## Scope

**Output file:** `docs/operations/incidents/postmortem-template.md`

**Content outline:**

The template itself should include (with guidance notes for each section):

1. **Incident summary** — title, date, severity, duration, author, participants
2. **Impact** — number of users affected, features impacted, SLA breach (yes/no), estimated revenue impact if applicable
3. **Timeline** — table with columns: time (UTC), event description, who took action
4. **Root cause analysis** — primary root cause, contributing factors
5. **Detection** — how the incident was detected (monitoring, user report, etc.), time-to-detection
6. **Response** — what worked well, what was slow or difficult
7. **Resolution** — what fixed it, how resolution was confirmed
8. **Action items** — table with: action, owner, due date, priority (required: at least one prevention action)
9. **Lessons learned** — what did the team learn? What monitoring/automation would have helped?
10. **Blameless culture note** — reminder that this is about systems, not individuals

Include a filled-in example section alongside the template.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Template exists at `docs/operations/incidents/postmortem-template.md`
- [ ] All 10 sections are present with guidance notes
- [ ] Action items table includes owner and due date columns
- [ ] Blameless culture statement is included
- [ ] A filled-in example post-mortem is provided for a fictional incident
- [ ] Reviewed and approved by the engineering lead
