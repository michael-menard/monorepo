**For AC-008 (Composite Index Ordering)**:
For composite indexes, order columns from most selective to least selective (highest cardinality first). Example: For queries filtering by story_id + state, story_id should be first column (UUIDs have higher cardinality than enum states). Follow pattern: `CREATE INDEX idx_name ON table (high_cardinality_col, low_cardinality_col)`.
